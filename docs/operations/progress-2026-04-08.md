## Major Corrections / Learnings

- The earlier assumption that Kalshi open-market collection was only on the order of a few hundred markets was wrong.
- The actual public market surface is much larger, so collection needed a real crawl engine, not a simple hourly one-shot sync.
- That is now addressed with checkpointing, chunking, and bounded orchestration.
